About GirlScript Ranchi

“Everything is easy in life if you start early, fail early, and get up early” - Anubha Maneshwar (Founder of GirlScript)
Girlscript Ranchi is the Ranchi chapter of the GirlScript Foundation, which was set up on 2nd June, 2017. It is a section-8 Non - Profit company registered under Government of India. We are a community who encourages and guide the beginners in tech. The name GirlScript because we support diversity. We believe in equality in tech and hence 50% of the seats in every meet-up, workshop and conference are reserved for women and LGBTQ. Our mission is to empower people and specially under-represented groups by educating them on technology and related necessary skills and to create local opportunity, growth and impact via tech education in every country around the world and to bring diversity in the tech domain.

Our Programs

Girlscript
India Summit

GirlScript India Summit is our Annual and biggest celebration of technology, talent, entrepreneurship and diversity. It's a Two Days Event including Hackathon, Tech-Talks, Workshops.

The Uplift Project

This new global project aims at helping everyone looking for opportunities to leverage their knowledge, skills, to higher levels. If you're a student wanting to learn tech stacks like HTML, React, AI, ML etc, or are skilled in these skills then this program is for all of you!

GS Cup

We are bringing you the GS Cup in which you will be awarded with points on a scale of contribution you have made to the community. And the person with the most points will be the winner of the most exciting GS Cup.

Young Scholar Contest

Young Scholars Contest is a 7-day online program to give your child a headstart in their tech career. An online contest will be held in the last two days where students would implement what they learnt and compete for attractive prizes.
